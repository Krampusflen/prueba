Welcome to Santo Domingo

Meet the tourist attractions of Las Rocas de Santo Domingo

Las Rocas de Santo Domingo is located in the province of San Antonio, Valparaíso Region. Its climate is Mediterranean and is characterized by morning cloudiness throughout the year. Coastal rainfall averages 450 mm annually, and occurs mainly in winter. It is approximately 112 km away from the capital. It is one of the most exclusive seaside resorts, with an urbanization whose layout follows the topography of the land. It has large summer houses, beautiful gardens, streets and tree-lined squares. It is characterized by being a quiet sector whose activity focuses on the beach...
